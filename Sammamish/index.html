  Kingco Tracker displays relavant information on the current standings for qualifying for the Kingco league meet (May 12 & 13), broken down by athlete and by event, as well as the outlook on the team competition at Kingco. There are 16 spots in each event for athletes at the Kingco League meet. These spots will be given to the 16 athletes with the best marks this season with a couple exceptions. For vertical jumps, you must be able to hit the official opening height in addition to being top 16. If you are not quite top 16, you may still get a spot if another athlete scratches an event. Scatches are very common (a few may drop) in the 400m, and 800m because they are hard to double with other events, and jumping events because the sprinters, who are often also the jumpers, fill up their four event limit fairly quickly.

  Districts Tracker displays information for the Bi-District meet against District 3 (May 19 & 20). Kingco shares berths to State with the leagues that make up District 3. Combined, the two districts get to send six athletes to State in each event. Those six will be the top six finishers at the Bi-District meet.

• Qualifying: To Qualify for Districts, you have to finish in the top five at Kingco. So the "Cutoff" listed is the current #5 mark in Kingco. The actual mark needed to qualify might be faster, might be slower. The spots go to whoever performs the best on the day!
