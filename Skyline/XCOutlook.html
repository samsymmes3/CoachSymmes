 Outlook

  Boys:

  This year, five teams from the Districts meet get to go to state. Issaquah and Eastlake both solidly take two of those spots, as they're easily the top two ranked teams in the state. But, those other three spots are up for grabs! In a hypothetical meet, we currently place sixth, but every team but those top two are very close!

  Girls:

  We are currently basically tied with Eastlake if we can get all of the track girls out. If not, the team falls to around the same rank as the boys, fighting for a spot at State.
