Skyline Spartan XC - Race Info

This is our race schedule we have so far for the 2024 Cross Country Season! A great thing with cross country is that the whole team gets to go to the invitationals, so everyone is expected to run the races on Saturdays. Some of the invitationals have upwards of 200 schools, but everyone is put in races with athletes of a similar ability level. Invitationals are a great opportunity to compete at your level.

Bellarmine Invitational - Saturday, Sep 14 - Tacoma

Bellarmine is a smaller Invitational, with a course that is only 2 miles. This is a great low-key way to start the season, get a first peek at what Invitationals are like, and get used to racing again.

Fort Steilacoom Invitational - Saturday, Sep 21 - Lakewood

Ft. Steilacoom is a medium-sized Invite. There are currently 76 teams registered. It is a tough course, but a great opporunity to practice courses that change, and aren't just like running a track. There is a very fast first mile, followed by two big hills, where you will have to practice downshifting up the hill, and hitting the gas hard on the way back down.

Schedule - All races run Boys first, then Girls. They will be on the course at the same time, 10 minutes apart.
