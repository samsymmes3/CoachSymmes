 Listed is everyone who ran cross country last year, with your cross country 5k PRs. If you click your name, you will see Coach Symmes' AI predictions for the 2024 season. These are based on how athletes across Washington State in the last 15 years, who posted similar times to you, ran in their following cross country season. For example, 10% can be interpreted as "10% of athletes who ran times like you, ran this time or better the following cross country season". These are just fun and informational, and maybe you'll end up like Eli and Abby did in track, and perform better than the top 0.1%!

 If your times look very similar for the Track and XC projections, that doesn't mean you didn't improve, it means you improved an expected amount. A few of you (especially Freshman) can see the huge leaps you made from cross country to track in the data! For most of you who have slower projections from track season than cross country, the amount slower is very small, and you are generally the more distance-oriented athletes, who should convert better coming back to the longer distance. If you did not run at an invitational in cross country last season, you will not have a projection. If your projection would be over 30 minutes, the data will not be accurate.
