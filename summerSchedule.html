Summer Schedule

We are looking forward to a fun and productive summer! This is when you build the fitness that will let you carry your speed for a full 5k. We will have runs at Beaver Lake Park, and occational other locations, weight room sessions, and will connect you with your upper classmen for planning of fun activities outside of running. Bring water, good running attire (trainers, athletic shirt, sweatshirt?, shorts), and a way to record your run (GPS watch or Strava App on your phone), especially if you are new.
