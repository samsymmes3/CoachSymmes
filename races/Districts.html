Bi-District Championship

The Bi-District Championship is our combined meet with Wesco to determine which teams qualify for the State Championship. This will be the top 10 teams from King, Snohomish, Skagit, Whatcom, San Juan, and Island Counties. At this time, we are unsure if the top five or six teams from this meet will qualify for State, but both of our teams will be in the running for those spots. (Seven times the number of team berths qualify individually. So either 35 or 42.) Last year it just wasn't our day on the boys side with six teams racing for the last two spots, and we finished fourth out of those six, but our girls dialed in and had a great race for third and a spot at State. Individual highlights from last year were many PRs, including Logan running 16:45 to qualify for State as an individual, and Vlad running 16:49 to be the first non-State Qualifier. On the girls side, Anna, Becca, and Sydney ran lights out finishing first, second, and fifth, with Anna and Becca's times being the fastest and fifth fastest ever by girls from the State of Washington in 16:43 and 17:07 respectively. *We have not been given any official information about this meet. All information here is based on past experience and talking to other coaches and people in KingCo Athletics.
