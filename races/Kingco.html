KingCo League Championship

The KingCo Championship is the first race of our Post-Season. The Top 9 boys and girls from each team in the league get to compete. If we do not make State, our finish here is the most important reflection of our team performance this season. The top 6 teams out of 13 get to move on to the Bi-District Championship the following week. Both our boys and girls should make it through this round pretty easily, but it will be a good trial run for the following week at our State Qualifying meet. Last year, both our boys and girls finished third, behind the teams that went on to get first and second in State on the girl's side, and the teams that finished first and fourth in State on the boy's side. *We have not been given any official information about this meet. All information here is based on past experience and talking to other coaches and people in KingCo Athletics.

Date - Friday, October 25

Location - Lake Sammamish State Park, Issaquah*
