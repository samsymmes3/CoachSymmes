State Championship

The State Championship. The Top 16 teams in the State racing for the podium. We would love to qualify with the team, as the team atmosphere traveling to the State Championship is unbeatable. Last year, our girls qualified as a team, and finished 7th (without Becca), and Reidar, Yash, and Logan qualified individually. The highlights were Anna winning her second Cross Country State title, Sydney with her second Top 10 finish at State, and the girls team still managing to be the 7th best team in the State without Becca.
