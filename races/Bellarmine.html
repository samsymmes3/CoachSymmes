Bellarmine Invitational

Bellarmine is a smaller Invitational, with a course that is only 2 miles. This is a great low-key way to start the season, and get a first experience of what Invitationals are like, and get used to racing again. Even though the meet is small (26 teams), there is still great competition with teams like Bishop Blanchet, Curtis, Sequim, Stadium, and Mercer Island also attending! Highlights from last year were watching Yash and Lucy win the freshman races, with Yash setting a new course record, and watching a very close varsity girls race, with second through eighth finishing within 40 points.

Date - Saturday, September 14

Location - Bellarmine High School, Tacoma
